Learning to play guitar is a an a wonderful pursuit. Learning to play like your favorite players can take years but learning to enjoy playing is very easy. With just a few minutes of regular practice per day, you will be well on your way to learning some of your favorite songs. This page is meant to teach and inspire players of all levels to grow and expand their skillset.
